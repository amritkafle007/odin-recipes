Burger

Description

With this black bean burger recipe, you will never want to eat frozen veggie burgers again! These are a quick, easy, and flavorful vegetarian delight.

Ingredients

  • Canned black beans\
  • Half a green bell pepper
  • Half an onion
  • Fresh garlic
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Thai chili sauce
  • A whole egg
  • Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly coat a sheet of aluminum foil with cooking spray.
  2. Mash black beans in a medium bowl with a fork until thick and pasty.
  3. Finely chop bell pepper, onion, and garlic in a food processor, then stir them into the mashed beans.
  4. Combine egg, chili powder, cumin, and chili sauce in a small bowl; stir into the bean mixture.
  5. Mix in bread crumbs until the mixture is sticky and holds together.
  6. Divide the mixture into four patties and place them on the prepared foil.
  7. Grill for about 8 minutes on each side.
