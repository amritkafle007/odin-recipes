Pizza

Description

This pesto pizza can be made with your favorite prepared pesto or homemade pesto. It's so quick to make for a light and flavorful alternative to regular cheese and tomato pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • ½ cup pesto
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 (2 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
  • ½ small red onion, chopped
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Spread pesto on the pizza crust. Top with tomato, bell pepper, olives, red onion, artichoke hearts, and feta cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and browned, about 8 to 10 minutes.
